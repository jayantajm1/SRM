# WhatsFlow CRM PRD

## Product Vision
WhatsFlow CRM is a WhatsApp-first CRM for Indian small businesses. WhatsApp is the primary customer interface, while the web app acts as the operations dashboard for the business owner and team.

## Problem Statement
Small businesses lose leads and customer context because conversations happen across WhatsApp, calls, and manual follow-ups without a central system.

## Target Users
- Gym
- Salon
- Tuition center
- Medical shop
- Clinic
- Restaurant
- Freelancer
- Furniture shop
- Real estate consultant
- Retail shop
- Travel agency

## MVP Goal
Deliver a focused SaaS starter that centralizes customer conversations, leads, reminders, and follow-up tasks around WhatsApp workflows.

## MVP Scope
- Authentication and role-based access
- Business profile setup
- Customer directory
- Conversation timeline linked to customers
- Follow-up tasks and reminders
- Basic lead pipeline
- Dashboard with key metrics
- WhatsApp integration abstraction
- Admin web dashboard
- Audit logging

## Out of Scope for MVP
- Full payments stack
- AI automation suite
- Broadcast campaigns at scale
- Multi-tenant billing engine
- Native mobile apps

## Success Metrics
- Faster follow-up completion
- Reduced missed leads
- Clear customer history in one place
- Simple daily workflow for business owners

## Core User Journeys
1. Owner signs in and configures business details.
2. Team adds a customer from a WhatsApp conversation.
3. User creates a follow-up reminder or task.
4. User views customer timeline and recent activity.
5. User tracks lead status from dashboard.

## Product Principles
- WhatsApp-first, not web-first
- Minimal clicks for daily operations
- Mobile-friendly admin UI
- Strong auditability and traceability
- Secure by default
